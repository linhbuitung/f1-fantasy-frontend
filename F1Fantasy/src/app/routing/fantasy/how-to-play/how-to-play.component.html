How to Play WooF1 Fantasy

Step 1: Register and Log In

  • Create an account using email or social login (OAuth supported).
  • Set up your profile and choose your favorites (1 constructor, 2 drivers).

Step 2: Build Your Team

  • Select 5 drivers and 2 constructors from the current F1 season.
  • Stay within the budget cap of $100.
  • Choose a captain whose points will be doubled.

Step 3: Join a League

  • Search for public leagues or create your own private league.
  • Private leagues require invitations or passwords.
  • Compete with friends or global players.

Step 4: Make Transfers

  • Adjust your lineup before each race.
  • You get 2 free transfers per race week.
  • Extra transfers cost 10 points each.
  • Transfers lock 1 hour before qualifying.

Step 5: Use Powerups

  • Use one powerup per race to boost your score.
  • Each powerup can be used once per season.
  • Available powerups:
    • DRS Enabled: Double points for one driver.
    • Free Transfer Week: No penalty for extra transfers.
    • Escapist: Negates negative points from drivers.

Step 6: Race Day & Scoring

  • After each race, scores are calculated based on:
    • Driver grid start and final position.
    • Fastest lap and positions gained/lost.
    • Constructor performance.
    • Captain bonus and powerup effects.
  • Negative scores apply for DNF statuses.

Step 7: Track Progress

  • View leaderboards for:
    • Global rankings.
    • League standings.
    • Driver and constructor stats.
  • Check your favorite drivers and constructor performance.

Step 8: Use AI Predictions

  • Earn AI usage by logging in daily (1 use per 14 consecutive days).
  • Use AI to predict:
    • Qualifying grid for the next race.
    • Main race results based on grid input.
  • AI uses historical data from jolpica-f1 API.

Tip: Plan your transfers and powerups strategically to maximize your score each week!
